# ICP: Service-based Businesses for Immediate Billing

- **Offer:** Implement an immediate billing system to streamline invoicing.
- **Outcome:** Faster and more accurate invoicing, leading to improved cash flow and more time for strategic work.
- **ICP:** Service-based businesses (e.g., marketing agencies, consulting firms) with 5-50 employees in North America.
- **Persona:** Operations Manager, who feels the pain of managing an increasingly complex billing process.
- **Trigger:** The company has recently hired several new people, making the existing manual billing process unsustainable.
- **Problem 1:** The influx of new hires is making the manual billing process even more time-consuming and error-prone.
- **Risk 1:** This leads to delayed or inaccurate invoices, which in turn causes cash flow problems and damages client relationships.
- **Problem 2:** The Operations Manager is spending too much time on administrative tasks instead of focusing on strategic initiatives.
- **Risk 2:** This hinders the company's ability to scale and grow efficiently.
- **Proof:** A testimonial from @barry-callebaut, who saved 4 hours per week.
- **Constraints:** unknown
