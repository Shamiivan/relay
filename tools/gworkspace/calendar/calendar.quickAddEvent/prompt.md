Use `calendar.quickAddEvent` to create events from natural language. Google parses the text to extract time, date, and title.

Examples:
- `"Team lunch Friday at noon"`
- `"Meeting with Bob 3pm-4pm tomorrow"`
- `"Dentist appointment April 15 at 2:30pm"`

Use `calendar.createEvent` instead when you need precise control over start/end times, attendees, reminders, or recurrence.
