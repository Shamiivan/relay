# Research for Email Campaign

## Target Audience

Growth-oriented accounting firms that are looking to expand their services to include fractional CFO advisory services. The target persona is a Partner or senior decision-maker within these firms.

## Pain Points

Based on web research, these firms are likely to be experiencing the following pain points:

* Difficulty in scaling their services and managing a growing team.
* Competition from other firms offering similar services.
* Need to differentiate their services from traditional accounting and bookkeeping.
* Pressure to adopt new technologies to improve efficiency and provide more value to clients.
* Struggle to balance the demands of client work with business development and marketing.

## Offer

The offer is to provide a solution that helps these firms to automate their accounting processes, freeing up their time to focus on high-value CFO advisory services. The solution will be positioned as a way to increase efficiency, reduce costs, and improve client satisfaction.

## Leads

I have identified 197 potential leads using Apollo. These are partners and senior-level individuals in accounting firms that are likely to be a good fit for the offer.
