# Alara

This directory holds the company context for Alara inside Relay.

## Discovery Brief

Alara is positioned as a workflow-systems business for owner-led companies with predictable operations.
The core belief is that many growing businesses think they have a hiring problem when they actually have a workflow problem.

The shift in framing is:

- old model: hire by role
- new model: decompose work by workflow and choose the right mix of human, software, or agent for each step

Mission:

- turn repeatable manual work into reliable systems

Core operating belief:

- the companies that scale cleanly move from role-based growth to workflow-based operations

## ICP

- owner-operators of growing businesses
- roughly 5 to 50 employees
- enough revenue and workflow volume that dropped tasks are expensive
- recurring handoffs, follow-up, approvals, and visibility gaps
- too much operational state still lives in the owner's phone, inbox, and memory

## Problem Framing

The recurring failures are:

- follow-up falls through
- approvals and handoffs stall on the owner
- visibility requires chasing
- recruiting and admin stay reactive

Working diagnosis:

- the owner is still the operating system
- the business has not yet been broken down to the workflow level

## Solution Framing

Alara builds the operational layer around existing workflows.
The value is not generic "AI automation". The value is workflow decomposition, then assigning each step to the right mix of human, software, or agent.

Common deliverable categories:

- follow-up systems
- recruiting systems
- approval and handoff automation
- dashboards and reporting
- customer-facing infrastructure when credibility matters

## Delivery Principles

- start narrow
- prove outcomes
- keep scope concrete
- do not pitch AI as magic
- define tasks with observable behavior
- keep the owner out of day-to-day project management
