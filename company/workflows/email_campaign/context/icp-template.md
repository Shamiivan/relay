# ICP Template

Capture only what is required to draft a relevant campaign.

Use the company brief first.

Before filling this out, use bash to read `company/<name>/README.md` and inherit:

- company positioning
- ICP hints
- solution framing
- delivery principles

The human should only need to supply what is still missing for this campaign.

Required fields:

- Offer: what is being sold in one sentence
- Outcome: what business result it creates
- ICP:
  - industry
  - geography
  - company size
  - budget sensitivity if known
  - technographic clues if relevant
  - immediate business goals
  - main operational pain points
  - common blockers to purchase
- Persona:
  - role title
  - why this person feels the problem
  - what responsibility makes the offer relevant to them
- Trigger: what observable signal suggests now is a good time to reach out
- Problem 1: main pain point
- Risk 1: what happens if they do nothing
- Problem 2: secondary pain point
- Risk 2: what happens if they do nothing
- Proof: one case study, testimonial, anecdote, or measurable result
- Constraints: anything relevant about volume, tone, exclusions, or compliance

Output format:

- concise bullets
- no placeholders left unresolved
- if a field is unknown, say `unknown`
