# Sequence Structure

Default sequence:

- 5 emails
- 18 days total
- plain text
- two major pain themes

Suggested structure:

1. Day 1: initial email focused on problem 1
2. Day 3: follow-up on problem 1 with a new angle
3. Day 9: initial email focused on problem 2
4. Day 12: follow-up on problem 2 with a new angle
5. Day 18: low-pressure closeout or value add

Suggested settings for later campaign setup:

- thread follow-ups where appropriate
- open tracking off
- link tracking off
- plain text on
- stop on reply on
- conservative volume per inbox
