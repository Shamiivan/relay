# Research Template

Use this file to structure discovery evidence before writing `research.md`.

Required sections:

- `# Campaign Research`
- `## Offer Hypothesis`
- `## Pain Signals`
- `## Trigger Signals`
- `## Apollo Segment Notes`
- `## Recommended Segment`

Section guidance:

## Offer Hypothesis

- one sentence version of the offer
- the business result it is supposed to create

## Pain Signals

- short bullets from web research
- prioritize recurring operational pain language
- include the source or domain where helpful

## Trigger Signals

- recent company-news or event-driven signals
- rank stronger signals above weaker ones
- favor recency and relevance

## Apollo Segment Notes

- what account pattern was searched
- what persona pattern was searched
- whether the results looked too narrow, too broad, or sensible

## Recommended Segment

- one proposed campaign segment
- persona
- trigger bucket
- why this segment is worth testing first
